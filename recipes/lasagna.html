Lasagna

Description

A delicious cheesy meal fit for around 4-6 people. Several layers of enticing mozzarella cheese, layered with ground meat and pasta.

Ingredients

  • Shredded cheese
  • Pasta layers
  • Pasta sauce
  • Ground meat
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Get a tray and put a layer of pasta strips
  2. Put a layer of ground meat
  3. Put a layer of pasta sauce
  4. Repeat steps 1-3 until almost filled to the top
  5. Put final layer of pasta strips
  6. Put a layer of cheese
  7. Put in the oven for an hour at 400 degrees fahrenheit
  8. Take out (carefully) and sprinkle a pinch of oregano (evenly) over the top
